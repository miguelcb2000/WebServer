ALTERATION HUNTING

A VR Game.

Game Story

There will be a collision between universes in the room!
Some items will be swapped with entities from another universe, and new items will appear.
You must remember which objects are in the room to detect objects that do not correspond to your reality.
Luckily, you have a portal gun to return them to their origin.
You must find and return these alterations to keep the multiverse stable!
